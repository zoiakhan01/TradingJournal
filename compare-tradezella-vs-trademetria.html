TradeZella includes a full trade replay feature that lets you re-watch your trades tick by tick with chart overlays, entry/exit markers, and performance annotations. You can view trade history in a table format and attach notes, but there is no interactive chart playback. This is a significant gap for active traders who rely on visual review.

The Bottom Line
Our Final Verdict

After extensive testing with real trade data, here is where each platform stands.

Our Pick
TradeZella
4.9

TradeZella is the better trading journal platform for traders who are serious about improving. Its combination of AI analytics, built-in trade replay, 500+ broker integrations, strategy playbooks, and a polished mobile experience makes it the most complete journal on the market. If you want a tool that actively helps you find patterns and improve, TradeZella is the clear choice.

For traders ready to invest in their growth, TradeZella delivers far more value per dollar.
