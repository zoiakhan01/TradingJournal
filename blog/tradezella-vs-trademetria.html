TradeZella vs Trademetria: Which Trading Journal Should You Choose in 2026?

March 2026·12 min read·By the TradingJournal.com team

Quick Verdict

TradeZella is the more powerful platform for traders serious about improvement — offering backtesting, trade replay, 25+ strategy templates, and 50+ analytics reports that Trademetria doesn't have. Trademetria is the better choice if you want a free plan, mobile apps right now, or gamification features to stay motivated.

Best for: TradeZella for comprehensive analysis and strategy development. Trademetria for budget-conscious traders who want mobile access and a free starting point.

Overview

TradeZella supports 500+ broker integrations while Trademetria supports 400+. They cover the major asset classes, but they target different trader profiles with very different feature sets.

TradeZella is built as an all-in-one trading development platform. It combines journaling, 50+ analytics reports, backtesting with 11+ years of historical data, trade replay with 3 modes, 25+ strategy templates from professional traders, Zella University education, and Spaces mentor tools — all in a single subscription. With a 4.8/5 Trustpilot rating, it has established itself as one of the most complete journal platforms available.

Trademetria, founded in 2016 with 80,000+ users, takes a more accessible approach. It offers a free plan (30 orders/month), native mobile apps, gamification through trading challenges, AI coaching on the Pro plan, and white-label tools for trading educators. At 4.0/5 on Trustpilot, it's well-regarded but positions itself as more of a starter journal than a comprehensive platform.

The core question is whether you need depth (TradeZella) or accessibility (Trademetria).

Detailed Comparison

Pricing

TradeZella offers Basic at $29/mo ($24/mo annually) and Premium at $49/mo ($33/mo annually). No free plan, no free trial.

Trademetria offers a free plan (30 orders/month), Basic at $29.95/mo, and Pro at $39.95/mo.

Trademetria's free plan is a genuine advantage for traders who aren't ready to commit financially. But at paid tiers, the feature gap makes TradeZella the better value. TradeZella Basic ($29/mo) includes backtesting, trade replay, 25+ strategy templates, 50+ reports, Zella University, and Spaces — features Trademetria doesn't have at any price point.

Analytics & Reports

TradeZella generates 50+ pre-built analytics reports covering every angle of trading performance — date/time analysis, price/quantity reports, risk analysis, options expiration, custom tag reports, and a compare tool. Every custom tag automatically creates its own report. The depth is unmatched in the journal space.

Trademetria offers standard analytics with basic reports on win rate, P&L breakdowns, instrument performance, and timing. The analytics are clean and functional but limited in scope compared to TradeZella's 50+ structured reports.

Backtesting

TradeZella includes unlimited backtesting on all plans with 11+ years of historical data back to September 2014, multi-chart layouts (up to 8), multi-symbol support (up to 5), built-in ICT indicators (FVG, Asian Session Range, HTF Bias, Key Levels, Power of 3), and full integration with strategy templates.

Trademetria has no backtesting. It offers a PnL Simulator that models different position sizes against past trades, but this isn't the same as testing strategies against historical market data. For traders who want to develop and validate strategies before risking real capital, TradeZella's backtesting is a major differentiator.

Trade Replay

TradeZella offers Trade Replay on all plans with three modes — Trade Replay (individual trades), Day Replay (entire sessions), and Scenario Replay (coming soon). Second-by-second playback with Level II data, Time of Sales, and integrated journaling.

Trademetria has no trade replay.

Strategy Tools

TradeZella provides 25+ ready-made strategy templates from professional traders, custom strategy template creation, strategy template performance tracking, missed trade logging, and shared strategy templates. This is a complete strategy management system.

Trademetria has no strategy templates. You can tag trades by setup, but there's no structured framework for documenting, testing, and tracking strategies.

AI Features

TradeZella includes Zella Insights (data-driven pattern recognition, behavioral analysis, risk warnings) with Zella AI (conversational) coming soon.

Trademetria includes AI Coach on the Pro plan ($39.95/mo) that analyzes patterns and provides recommendations.

Both offer AI-powered insights, but TradeZella's analytics are deeper and power the 50+ report system. Trademetria's AI Coach is useful but less comprehensive.

Mobile Experience

Trademetria has native iOS and Android apps — a clear advantage today for traders who review trades on the go.

TradeZella is web-based with responsive mobile access and a native app coming soon. Until the app launches, Trademetria wins on mobile.

Mentorship

TradeZella's Spaces lets mentors view students' full accounts, monitor P&L with color-coded charts, leave trade-specific feedback, and manage unlimited students. It's a structured coaching platform.

Trademetria offers white-label admin dashboards for trading educators to create branded monitoring. Different approach — Trademetria is better for building a branded teaching business, while TradeZella is better for actual coaching functionality.

Education

TradeZella includes Zella University with bootcamps, webinars, and structured learning content at no extra cost.

Trademetria has no education platform.

Gamification

Trademetria offers trading challenges, achievements, and community competitions. TradeZella does not have gamification features.

For traders motivated by competition and milestones, Trademetria's gamification is a unique feature. For traders focused on data-driven improvement, TradeZella's analytical tools are more valuable.

Who Should Choose Which

Choose TradeZella if you want the most comprehensive trading journal available, need backtesting with historical data, want trade replay to review execution, value strategy templates from professional traders, want structured education, work with a mentor, or want 50+ analytics reports for deep performance analysis.

Choose Trademetria if you want a free journal to start with, need mobile apps right now, are motivated by gamification and challenges, want to build a branded trading community (white-label), are a beginner who wants a low-commitment starting point, or trade CFDs (which TradeZella doesn't list as a separate asset class).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Trademetria free?

Yes — 30 orders per month on the free plan. TradeZella has no free plan or trial.

Which has better backtesting?

TradeZella — unlimited backtesting on all plans with 11+ years of data, ICT indicators, and strategy template integration. Trademetria has no backtesting (only a PnL Simulator).

Which has better mobile apps?

Trademetria — native iOS and Android apps available now. TradeZella's mobile app is coming soon.

Which is better for beginners?

Trademetria is more accessible (free plan, mobile apps). TradeZella offers more learning resources (Zella University, 25+ strategy templates) for beginners who want structured development.

Does Trademetria have trade replay?

No. TradeZella offers trade replay on all plans with 3 modes.

Which has better AI?

TradeZella's Zella Insights powers deeper analytics (50+ reports) and has conversational Zella AI coming soon. Trademetria's AI Coach provides useful recommendations but is less comprehensive.

Which is better for prop firm trading?

TradeZella — it connects to prop firm platforms and has Prop Firm Sync Mode coming soon. Trademetria doesn't have dedicated prop firm tools.

Which has more broker integrations?

TradeZella supports 500+ brokers while Trademetria supports 400+. TradeZella has 100 more broker integrations, but both cover virtually all major retail platforms.

Is TradeZella worth the extra cost over Trademetria's free plan?

If you backtest strategies, replay trades, use strategy templates, or want structured education — yes. The features TradeZella includes at $29/mo would cost significantly more if purchased as separate tools. If you only need basic trade logging, Trademetria's free plan works fine.

Which has better mentorship tools?

Different strengths. TradeZella's Spaces provides better coaching functionality (full account access, trade-level feedback, unlimited students). Trademetria's white-label dashboards are better for building a branded teaching business.
