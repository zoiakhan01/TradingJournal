# TradeZella Trading Journal & Analytics

Automatically sync your trades from 400+ brokers, analyze performance with powerful reports, and understand where your edge lives and where it's leaking.

## Dashboard

A real-time snapshot of your trading performance the moment you log in.

- **Performance Calendar** — See wins, losses, and breakeven days at a glance with a customizable calendar view
- **Zella Score** — A 0–100 metric measuring overall trading health factoring in profitability, risk management, consistency, and discipline
- **Multiple Accounts** — Manage and view multiple trading accounts in one dashboard
- **Customizable Widgets** — Switch between layouts and add widgets for the metrics you care about

## Automatic Trade Import

Connect your broker and every trade syncs instantly — no manual entry.

- **Auto-Sync From Brokers** — 400+ broker integrations including MetaTrader 4/5, NinjaTrader, ThinkorSwim, Interactive Brokers, Tradovate, cTrader, DXtrade, TradeLocker, Robinhood, Bybit, and more
- **TradingView Charts** — Review trades on professional-grade charts without leaving the platform
- **Multiple TP/SL Tracking** — Log all profit targets and stop losses to compare planned vs. actual risk-reward
- **Running P&L** — See the full story of each trade — drawdowns, early entries, late exits
- **MAE/MFE Analysis** — See how far trades moved against you and in your favor to refine entries and exits
- **Default Commission & Fees** — Set your broker's fees once and see true net P&L on every trade

## Trade Tagging & Notes

Organize and annotate every trade for deeper review.

- **Strategy Tagging** — Link each trade to a specific strategy to track what's actually working
- **Custom Tags** — Tag trades by setup, emotion, mistake, or any custom category
- **Trade Notes** — Add reflections and context to any trade for review later
- **Screenshots & Attachments** — Attach charts, images, or files to any trade for visual context
- **Trade Rating System** — Rate each trade to quickly filter for best and worst executions

## Analytics & Reports

50+ reports that show you exactly where you're winning and losing.

- **Performance Summary** — Best and worst performing tags, most used tags, and highest win rate tags at a glance
- **Customizable Charts** — Plot multiple metrics (Net P&L, Avg Drawdown, Trade Count) in a single view. Choose from 30+ metrics across profitability, risk, time analysis, and streaks
- **Cross Analysis** — Apply secondary filters to see how setups, mistakes, or emotions perform across different conditions (day of week, time, symbol)
- **Filter by Anything** — Slice data by strategy, symbol, tags, day & time, risk, or wins vs. losses

## Strategy Builder

Create, track, and measure your trading strategies.

- **Build Custom Strategies** — Define setups with clear rules, criteria, and parameters
- **Pre-Built Strategies** — Choose from proven strategies created by verified profitable traders
- **Tag Strategies to Trades** — Link strategies to individual trades for automatic performance tracking
- **Measure Performance** — Compare strategies side-by-side with win rate, net P&L, avg win/loss, and trade count

## Trading Habits & Routines

Tools to build consistent, disciplined trading habits.

- **Daily Trading Checklist** — Run through your pre-session checklist before every session
- **Pre-Market Prep Templates** — Structure your morning routine with built-in templates
- **Economic Calendar** — High-impact news events so you know what's moving the markets
- **End-of-Day Summary** — Close each session with a summary of the day's performance
- **Consistency Heatmap** — Monitor your trading discipline over time with a visual tracker

## Notebook

Document observations, lessons, and market notes in one place.

- **Journal Templates** — Pre-built templates for trade reviews, weekly recaps, and more
- **Trade-Linked Notes** — Attach detailed notes to individual trades for context during review
- **Organized Folders** — Sort notes by topic, strategy, or timeframe
- **Session Recaps** — Select a date range to review and journal weekly, monthly, or quarterly performance

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is TradeZella's Trading Journal?**
TradeZella automatically imports trades from your connected broker and analyzes performance across dozens of metrics. It includes a customizable dashboard, trade log, daily journal, notebook, and detailed reports.

**Which brokers does TradeZella support?**
400+ brokers including MetaTrader 4/5, NinjaTrader, ThinkorSwim, Interactive Brokers, Tradovate, cTrader, DXtrade, TradeLocker, Robinhood, Bybit, and more. Connect via auto-sync, file upload, or manual entry. See all at [Supported Brokers](https://www.tradezella.com/brokersupport).

**Does TradeZella support prop firms?**
Yes. If TradeZella supports the broker or platform your prop firm uses (Tradovate, Rithmic, DXtrade, TradeLocker), you can sync and journal prop firm trades like any other account.

**What reports are included?**
P&L, win rate, profit factor, day/time analysis, symbol breakdown, win vs. loss comparisons, risk metrics, drawdown tracking, and performance by strategy or custom tags.

**What is the Zella Score?**
A 0–100 metric measuring overall trading performance — factoring in profitability, risk management, consistency, and discipline beyond just P&L.

**Does TradeZella work for stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto?**
Yes. Track multiple accounts across all asset classes in one place, with dedicated options analytics and the ability to filter reports by account or market.
