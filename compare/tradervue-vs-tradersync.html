The Bottom Line
Our Final Verdict

After testing both platforms side by side with real trade data, here is where we landed.

Our Pick
TraderSync — by a slim margin
4.0

TraderSync wins on features — trade replay, a modern interface, an iOS app, and slightly better broker support. It is the more complete journal of the two and the better choice for traders who want a polished experience without paying premium prices. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tradervue or TraderSync better for day trading?

TraderSync is significantly better for day trading. It offers AI-powered analytics (AI Composites), trade replay, 200+ performance reports, and risk management tools. Tradervue provides basic trade logging and simple analytics but lacks AI features, backtesting, and advanced performance tools that active day traders need.

Does Tradervue have a free plan?

Yes. Tradervue offers a free tier allowing up to 30 trades per month with basic import and community access. TraderSync does not have a free plan (starts at $29.95/month). For traders on a tight budget who just need basic trade logging, Tradervue's free plan is its strongest advantage.

How many brokers does TraderSync support vs Tradervue?

TraderSync supports 950+ broker integrations, the highest count among trading journals. Tradervue supports approximately 80+ brokers. TraderSync covers over 11x more brokers and platforms, giving it a commanding lead in compatibility.

Does TraderSync have AI features?

Yes. TraderSync's standout AI feature is AI Composites, which automatically analyzes your trade history to identify recurring patterns, generates predictive overlays, and highlights opportunities for improvement. Tradervue has no AI-powered analysis, pattern recognition, or automated insights.

What is the price difference between Tradervue and TraderSync?

Tradervue offers Free, Silver ($29.95/month), and Gold ($49.95/month) plans. TraderSync offers Pro ($29.95/month), Premium ($79.95/month), and Elite ($199.95/month). While both start at similar prices, TraderSync provides dramatically more features at each tier, including AI analytics, trade replay, and 200+ reports.

What are the Trustpilot ratings for Tradervue vs TraderSync?

TraderSync has a 4.7/5 rating on Trustpilot, while Tradervue has a 2.6/5 rating. The significant gap reflects differences in feature development, user experience, platform investment, and ongoing support quality.

Does Tradervue support crypto trading?

No. Tradervue supports stocks, options, futures, and forex but does not support cryptocurrency. TraderSync supports crypto alongside all other asset classes through its 950+ broker integrations. Crypto traders should choose TraderSync.

Which trading journal has trade replay — Tradervue or TraderSync?

TraderSync offers trade replay on Premium and Elite plans, allowing traders to step through their trades on charts and review entries and exits visually. Tradervue does not offer trade replay in any form. For visual trade review, TraderSync is the only option between the two.
