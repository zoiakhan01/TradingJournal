Frequently Asked Questions

Is TradeZella or Trademetria better for beginners?

Both are beginner-friendly, but in different ways. Trademetria offers a free plan and a clean, simple interface that won't overwhelm new traders. TradeZella provides structured learning through Zella University with video courses, 25+ strategy templates, and mentorship tools. If budget is the priority, Trademetria's free tier is a good starting point. If learning to trade properly matters most, TradeZella's education features give beginners a faster path to improvement.

Does Trademetria have a free plan?

Yes — Trademetria offers a free plan with basic trade logging, limited reports, and support for manual trade entry. It's one of the few trading journals with a functional free tier. TradeZella does not currently offer a free plan but provides significantly more features starting at $29/month.

How many brokers does TradeZella support compared to Trademetria?

TradeZella supports 500+ broker integrations across stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto. Trademetria supports approximately 400+ brokers. Both have strong broker coverage, though TradeZella has a slight edge and includes more direct API connections for automatic trade syncing.

Does Trademetria have backtesting or trade replay?

No. Trademetria does not offer backtesting or trade replay. TradeZella includes both — unlimited backtesting with 11+ years of historical data and trade replay with multiple modes. These are significant differentiators for traders who want to test strategies and review their executions visually.

What is the price difference between TradeZella and Trademetria?

Trademetria offers Free, Basic ($19.99/month), and Premium ($29.99/month) plans. TradeZella starts at $29/month (Essential) with higher tiers at $49/month and $79/month. Trademetria is more affordable, especially with its free tier. TradeZella costs more but includes backtesting, trade replay, strategy templates, and education that Trademetria doesn't offer.

Which trading journal is better for options trading?

TradeZella has stronger options support with dedicated options expiration reports, strategy templates for options strategies, and backtesting capabilities. Trademetria supports options as an asset class and provides basic P&L tracking but lacks options-specific analytics and tools.

What are the Trustpilot ratings for TradeZella vs Trademetria?

TradeZella has a 4.8/5 rating on Trustpilot, while Trademetria has a 4.0/5 rating. TradeZella's higher rating reflects its more comprehensive feature set and active development, while Trademetria's solid 4.0 reflects a reliable if more basic trading journal experience.

Does Trademetria support crypto trading?

Yes — both Trademetria and TradeZella support cryptocurrency trading. Trademetria integrates with several crypto exchanges, and TradeZella covers crypto through its 500+ broker integrations. Both are viable options for crypto traders.
