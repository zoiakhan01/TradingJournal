What Is TradeZella?

TradeZella is a cloud-based trading journal and analytics platform that brings everything a trader needs into one place. Instead of using a spreadsheet for journaling, a separate app for charting, another tool for backtesting, and Discord for mentor check-ins -- TradeZella handles all of it. Your live trades, backtested trades, strategy frameworks, replay sessions, mentor feedback, and performance analytics all live in the same ecosystem.

The platform connects to over 500 brokers and platforms -- Interactive Brokers, MetaTrader 4 and 5, cTrader, TradeLocker, NinjaTrader, Tradovate, ThinkorSwim, Schwab, Robinhood, and many more. Trades sync automatically once connected. It supports stocks, options, futures, forex, and cryptocurrencies.

TradeZella holds a 4.8/5 Trustpilot rating -- the highest in the trading journal category, ahead of TraderSync (4.7/5), Edgewonk (4.7/5), Trademetria (4.0/5), and Tradervue (2.6/5).

Key Features

Customizable Analytics Dashboard

The dashboard is fully customizable -- you choose which widgets to display, how to arrange them, and what metrics to prioritize. It includes advanced analytics widgets you can rearrange, automatic price charts with your executions plotted directly on the chart, a calendar view showing daily P&L at a glance, equity curves and profitability charts, running P&L visualization that shows how your profit/loss fluctuated during each trade, and key KPIs like win rate, profit factor, and expectancy.

The running P&L feature is worth highlighting. Most journals show you the end result -- you made $200 or lost $150. TradeZella shows what happened during the trade. Maybe you were up $500 before giving back $300. That granular data reveals emotional pressure points that flat P&L numbers hide.

Advanced Trade Tracking & Journaling

Every trade that syncs is automatically logged with full details -- entry/exit prices, position size, P&L, timestamps, duration, commissions, and more.

Tags & Custom Categories. Tag every trade with setups, mistakes, market conditions, or anything else. Create custom tag categories based on your trading style. Tags feed directly into your reports -- so you can see that your "FOMO entries" average -2.1% while your "planned setups" average +1.8%. No other journal makes custom tag analytics this seamless.

Advanced Global Filter. Slice your data by any criteria -- strategy, symbol, tags, day, time, risk, wins vs. losses. The filter applies across all reports and analytics. Want to see how your breakout strategy performs on Mondays during the first hour? Set the filter and every report updates instantly.

Notebook with Templates. Built-in notebook for trading plans, watchlists, daily recaps, and pre-market notes. Use pre-built templates or create your own. Notes sync with your trading stats.

Risk Management Tools. Enter TP and SL levels, and TradeZella automatically calculates risk per trade, R-multiple, and risk-reward ratio.

MFA/MFE, Zella Scale & Best Exit Analysis

These are advanced metrics that most journals don't offer. They answer questions that standard P&L tracking can't.

MFA & MFE (Maximum Favorable/Adverse Excursion). Shows the maximum profit and maximum drawdown during each trade -- not just the final number. If your average MFE is $800 but your average realized profit is $300, you're consistently leaving $500 on the table. If your average MAE is -$400 but your stop is -$200, your trades are dipping deeper than your plan accounts for.

Zella Scale. Unique to TradeZella. Compares your actual P&L against your potential P&L, giving you a trading efficiency score. Are you capturing 40% of available moves or 80%? The Zella Scale tells you. Over time, improvements in your Zella Scale indicate genuine skill development, even when raw P&L fluctuates.

Best Exit Analysis. Analyzes your trade exits and calculates what your P&L would have been with different exit strategies. It answers "what if I had exited here instead?" using your actual trade data. You might discover that trailing by 1 ATR would have improved your overall P&L by 15%.

No other trading journal offers the Zella Scale or Best Exit Analysis. These metrics alone provide insights that would require hours of spreadsheet work to calculate manually.

50+ Drilled-Down Reports

This is where TradeZella separates itself from every competitor. The platform generates over 50 pre-built reports. For comparison, TraderSync offers 20--30 widgets, Tradervue's free plan has about 5, and Edgewonk offers 50+ stats but with a different approach.

Date & Time Reports -- analyze by day of week, time of day, week, month, trade duration, and session. Discover that you're profitable on Tuesdays but consistently lose on Fridays, or that your best trades happen in the first hour.

Price & Quantity Reports -- performance by symbol, price level, position size, and volume. See which instruments you trade best and where your sweet spot is for sizing.

Risk Reports -- R-multiple analysis, position size impact, risk-adjusted returns.

Options Expiration Report -- dedicated analytics for options traders tracking expiration-related performance.

Custom Tag Reports -- every custom tag category you create automatically generates its own report. Tag trades with "breakout," "reversal," or "gap fill" and get full performance stats for each.

Wins vs. Losses -- side-by-side comparison of winning and losing trade characteristics.

Compare -- generate reports comparing any two sets of trades. Morning vs. afternoon. Trending vs. ranging markets. Last month vs. this month.

Strategy Templates

TradeZella's strategy system lets you document, organize, and track your trading strategies with full performance analytics. The feature was recently renamed from "playbooks" to "strategies."

25+ Ready-Made Strategy Templates. This is a feature no other journal offers. TradeZella provides 25+ strategy templates created by professional traders -- including strategies from traders like Umar Ashraf, Anthony Crudele, Alex Temiz, and others. Templates cover stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto, with approaches ranging from ICT-based strategies to volume profile, break and retest, auction market theory, and more. Each includes the complete framework: overview, rules, entry/exit criteria, and example trades.

Create Your Own Strategies. Define entry criteria, exit rules, risk parameters, and market conditions. Tag trades to strategies and track performance over time -- win rate, net P&L, average win/loss, R-multiple, and more. Over time, this shows you exactly which strategies make money and which need work.

Log Missed Trades. Track setups that fit your strategy criteria but you didn't take. Over a month, you might discover that missed trades would have been your most profitable -- meaning fear or distraction is costing you money.

Shared Strategies. Share your best-performing strategies with other traders. Useful for trading groups, mentorship programs, and building communities around proven approaches.

Strategy Analytics. See your best and worst performing strategies side-by-side. Quickly identify which to double down on and which to retire.

For traders still developing their edge, the 25+ templates provide a structured starting point. For experienced traders, they offer proven frameworks to test and adapt. No other journal -- TraderSync, Edgewonk, Tradervue, or Trademetria -- offers anything like this.

Backtesting Engine

TradeZella's backtesting is one of the most comprehensive in the trading journal category -- and it's included on all plans with unlimited sessions. Most competing journals (Edgewonk, Tradervue, Trademetria) don't offer backtesting at all. TraderSync has systematic backtesting but it's rule-based rather than visual/chart-based.

11+ Years of Historical Data. Test strategies against data going back to September 2014 across forex, futures, stocks, and crypto. Multiple bull markets, bear markets, COVID crash, Fed rate cycles -- over a decade of market conditions.

Execute Like You Would Live. Place market, limit, and stop orders with realistic execution. Automatic position sizing by risk percentage. TP/SL by price, USD, or percentage. Auto-breakeven when price hits a target. Manual partials at 25%, 50%, 75%, or custom amounts.

Professional-Grade Charting. Multi-symbol support (up to 5 per session), multi-chart layouts (up to 8 charts simultaneously), multiple timeframes down to the second, and an economic calendar on your chart.

Built-In ICT Indicators. TradeZella is the only backtesting platform with native ICT indicators -- Fair Value Gap (FVG), Asian Session Range, HTF Bias, Key Levels, and Power of 3. No third-party scripts needed. For the growing ICT community, this is a major differentiator.

Go-To Feature. Skip forward to specific dates, candles, or trading sessions instantly. No waiting through dead periods.

Speed Control. Run backtests at 0.5x, 1x, 2x, 5x, or 10x speed.

Integrated Journaling. Every backtested trade is automatically logged to your journal with full details. Add notes, tags, and link strategies during sessions. Backtesting data flows directly into your analytics alongside live trades -- so you can compare backtested performance vs. live performance for the same strategy.

Advanced Execution Tools. Drag-and-drop SL/TP on chart, quick buy/sell buttons, hide orders for unbiased review, saved chart settings between sessions, and RTH/ETH data for stocks and futures.

The fact that backtesting is unlimited on all plans -- not locked behind premium -- is a significant advantage. Many platforms treat backtesting as a premium feature. TradeZella includes it as core.

Trade Replay

Trade Replay is different from backtesting. Backtesting tests hypothetical strategies on historical data. Trade Replay rewatches trades you actually took with real money. It's like reviewing game film.

Three Replay Modes:

Trade Replay -- select any trade from your journal and watch it unfold from pre-entry to final exit. See every tick that led to your decisions.

Day Replay -- replay your entire trading session. Understand how your first loss affected your second entry, or whether your discipline deteriorated as the day went on.

Scenario Replay (Coming Soon) -- scenario-based review for comparing similar setups and building pattern recognition.

Replay Controls: Second-by-second playback at any interval, speed control at 1x/2x/10x/20x/30x, entry and exit markers on chart, multiple timeframe views, jump to any execution point, Time of Sales data, and Level II market depth.

Integrated Journaling During Replay. Tag mistakes as you spot them, add notes, capture screenshots. Everything syncs to your reports.

Trade Replay is available on all plans. For comparison, TraderSync also offers replay across all tiers with up to 250ms precision on Elite -- which is more granular for scalpers. But TradeZella's Day Replay mode (reviewing entire sessions, not just individual trades) is unique and valuable for understanding session-level patterns.

Spaces (Mentor Mode)

Spaces transforms trading mentorship from guesswork into data-driven coaching. No other trading journal offers anything comparable -- TraderSync has no mentor tools, Edgewonk has none, Tradervue has basic sharing, and Trademetria has limited options.

For Mentors: Monitor students with color-coded P&L charts. Access a full view of any student's TradeZella account -- Dashboard, Trade Log, Notebook, Strategies, and Reports. Leave targeted feedback directly on specific journal entries. Create organized coaching folders. Access historical data back to when the student joined. Manage unlimited students on Premium (5 on Basic).

For Students: Privacy controls to choose which accounts to share. See mentor feedback directly on your trades. Revoke access anytime with one click.

Both mentor and student need active subscriptions. Basic includes 5 mentor invites; Premium is unlimited.

Zella University

An integrated education hub included at no extra cost with all plans.

Content includes structured learning bootcamps from beginner to advanced, trader development webinars, lessons on recovering from trading losses, and a library covering journaling best practices, risk management, and trading psychology.

What makes Zella University different from watching YouTube is the integration with the platform. When a lesson covers strategy development, you can build a strategy template right after. When it teaches backtesting, you can practice immediately. Education and tools in the same ecosystem.

TraderSync has limited educational content beyond AI coaching. Edgewonk, Tradervue, and Trademetria offer no education platform.

Coming Soon

Zella AI. Conversational AI that lets you ask questions about your trading, detect patterns, and get personalized insights. This will add interactive AI capabilities on top of the existing analytics. Separate from Zella Insights (the current analytics feature).

Prop Firm Sync Mode. Connect all prop firm accounts into one dashboard. Manage multiple evaluations, track performance across firms, handle payouts. For traders juggling multiple prop firm accounts, this is a major addition.

Scenario Replay. Third mode for Trade Replay. Scenario-based review for pattern recognition across similar setups.

Mobile App. Native mobile app in development. Currently web-responsive on mobile browsers. TraderSync and Trademetria both offer mobile apps today -- this is one of TradeZella's few gaps.

This is one of the most feature-rich "basic" plans in the journal space. Most platforms lock backtesting, replay, or advanced analytics behind premium.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TradeZella worth the price?

For traders who actively use the platform's tools -- backtesting, trade replay, strategies, and analytics -- the value is strong. A standalone backtesting platform alone can cost $30--$100/month. Add education subscriptions and separate analytics tools, and you're easily above $100/month. TradeZella bundles everything starting at $24/month on annual billing. If you only need basic trade logging, cheaper options exist. But for comprehensive trader development, it's the best all-in-one value.

Does TradeZella have a free trial?

No. This is the platform's most cited drawback. Start with monthly Basic at $29 and treat the first month as your evaluation period. If it delivers value, switch to annual billing.

How many brokers does TradeZella support?

Over 500 brokers and platforms, including Interactive Brokers, MetaTrader 4/5, cTrader, TradeLocker, NinjaTrader, Tradovate, ThinkorSwim, Schwab, Robinhood, Webull, and many more. New integrations are added regularly.

Does TradeZella have a mobile app?

Not yet -- a mobile app is in development. The web platform is responsive on mobile browsers, but a dedicated app will improve the experience. TraderSync and Trademetria both offer iOS and Android apps if mobile is a priority right now.

Is TradeZella good for beginners?

Yes. Zella University provides structured education from beginner to advanced. The 25+ strategy templates give new traders proven frameworks to study, backtest, and adapt. The dashboard is customizable, so beginners can start simple and add complexity as they grow.

Can I use TradeZella for prop firm trading?

Yes. TradeZella connects to all major prop firm platforms. Prop Firm Sync Mode is coming soon -- connect all prop firm accounts into one dashboard, manage multiple evaluations, and track payouts.

Does TradeZella support crypto?

Yes. Stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto. Backtesting data includes crypto pairs going back to September 2014.

What is Trade Replay and how is it different from backtesting?

Trade Replay rewatches your actual trades as they happened -- second by second with full market data, Time of Sales, and Level II. It's for reviewing real trades. Backtesting tests hypothetical strategies on historical data. Both are included on all plans.

What are strategies in TradeZella?

Strategies (formerly called playbooks) are structured frameworks where you define entry criteria, exit rules, risk parameters, and market conditions. Tag trades to strategies and track performance over time. TradeZella provides 25+ templates from professional traders, or create your own.

Does TradeZella have backtesting?

Yes -- unlimited backtesting on all plans with 11+ years of historical data. Multi-chart layouts (up to 8), multi-symbol support (up to 5), built-in ICT indicators, realistic order execution, auto-breakeven, partial closes, speed control, and full integration with strategies and journal.

What is the Zella Scale?

A proprietary metric that compares your actual P&L against potential P&L for each trade. It gives you a trading efficiency score -- showing whether you're capturing 40%, 60%, or 80% of available moves. Improvements over time indicate genuine skill development. No other journal has this.

How does Spaces (Mentor Mode) work?

Mentors connect with students for data-driven coaching. Mentors view students' full accounts -- Dashboard, Trade Log, Strategies, Notebook, and all 50+ Reports. Monitor multiple students with color-coded P&L charts and leave feedback on specific trades. Basic includes 5 mentor connections; Premium is unlimited.

What is Zella AI?

An upcoming conversational AI feature. Ask questions about your trading data, get pattern detection, and receive personalized insights. Separate from Zella Insights (the current analytics feature).

Does TradeZella have ICT indicators?

Yes -- the only trading journal with built-in ICT indicators in backtesting. Fair Value Gap (FVG), Asian Session Range, HTF Bias, Key Levels, and Power of 3. Native to the platform, no scripts needed.

What is the best TradeZella plan?

For most traders, Basic ($29/mo or $24/mo annually) includes everything -- all reports, backtesting, trade replay, strategies, Zella University, Spaces with 5 invites, and all advanced metrics. Premium ($49/mo or $33/mo annually) adds unlimited mentor invites. Only upgrade if you're a coach managing many students.

How does TradeZella compare to spreadsheets?

Spreadsheets give full customization but require manual entry and formula building. TradeZella automates trade logging through 500+ broker integrations, provides 50+ pre-built reports, and includes backtesting, trade replay, strategy tracking, and education that no spreadsheet can replicate. For traders spending more than 15 minutes per day on manual journaling, TradeZella saves time while providing deeper insights.
