Trademetria Review 2026: Best Free Trading Journal for Mobile Traders?

March 2026 · 10 min read · By the TradingJournal.com team

Quick Verdict

Trademetria is a solid trading journal that stands out for its freemium model, mobile apps, gamification features, and AI coaching. With 80,000+ users and a 4.0/5 Trustpilot rating, it offers an accessible entry point for traders who want more than a spreadsheet but aren't ready for premium pricing.

The platform's strengths are its free plan (30 orders/month), native iOS and Android apps, PnL simulator, and white-label tools for trading communities. Its weaknesses are the absence of backtesting, trade replay, and the depth of analytics that more comprehensive platforms provide.

Best for: Budget-conscious traders, mobile-first users, and trading community managers

Price range: Free -- $39.95/month

Verdict: A good starter journal with mobile apps and a free plan. Traders who outgrow the basics will eventually need a more comprehensive platform.

What Is Trademetria?

Trademetria was founded in 2016 by Thiago Ghilardi and has grown to serve 80,000+ users tracking over 15 million trades. The platform takes an accessible, community-focused approach -- offering a free tier that lets traders start journaling without financial commitment, paired with mobile apps for on-the-go trade logging.

Trademetria differentiates itself through gamification (trading challenges and achievements), AI-powered coaching on the Pro plan, and white-label tools that let trading educators create branded dashboards for their communities. It supports the widest range of asset classes in the journal space -- stocks, options, futures, forex, crypto, and CFDs.

Key Features

Free Plan (30 Orders/Month)

Trademetria's free tier allows up to 30 orders per month with basic import and standard analytics. For casual traders or those evaluating journal platforms, it's a no-commitment way to start. The free plan includes access to basic reports and the mobile app.

Mobile Apps (iOS & Android)

Trademetria offers native mobile apps that let you log trades, review analytics, and track performance from your phone. For traders who want to journal on the go, this is a meaningful advantage -- most trading journals are desktop-only.

The mobile experience isn't as polished or feature-complete as TraderSync's mobile apps, but it's functional and covers the basics well.

AI Coach (Pro Plan)

The Pro plan includes an AI Coach that analyzes your trading patterns and provides personalized recommendations. It identifies tendencies in your trading behavior and suggests adjustments based on your historical data.

The AI Coach is less interactive than TraderSync's Cypher AI (which offers conversational coaching) and less analytically deep than TradeZella's Zella Insights (which powers 50+ reports). But at $39.95/month, it provides useful guidance for traders who want AI-assisted improvement.

Gamification & Challenges

Trademetria incorporates gamification -- trading challenges, achievements, and community competitions -- to keep traders engaged and accountable. This is a unique approach in the journal space and appeals to traders who are motivated by competition and milestones.

PnL Simulator

A unique feature that lets you simulate different position sizes and scenarios against your historical trades to see how changes would have affected your P&L. Want to know what your results would look like if you'd used 2x position size on your winning setups? The PnL simulator shows you.

This is different from true backtesting (which lets you test strategies against historical market data). The PnL simulator works with trades you've already taken, while backtesting tests hypothetical strategies on historical charts. For full backtesting capability, platforms like TradeZella offer unlimited sessions with 11+ years of data.

White-Label Admin Dashboards

Trademetria offers white-label tools for trading educators and community managers to create branded admin dashboards. This lets coaches monitor their students' performance under their own brand -- useful for trading educators building a business around their mentorship.

It's a different approach from TradeZella's Spaces (Mentor Mode), which provides mentorship tools within the existing platform rather than white-label branding.

How Trademetria Compares

Trademetria occupies a middle ground in the trading journal market. It offers more than Tradervue (mobile apps, AI coaching, more modern interface) but less than TradeZella (no backtesting, no replay, no strategy templates, no education) or TraderSync (less sophisticated AI, less precise replay).

Its sweet spot is the budget-conscious trader who wants a free or low-cost journal with mobile access and basic AI coaching. Trading community managers may also find value in the white-label admin dashboards.

For traders who want comprehensive tools for serious improvement -- backtesting with 11+ years of data, trade replay, 25+ professional strategy templates, and structured education -- TradeZella provides a more complete ecosystem at a comparable price point.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Trademetria free?

Yes -- the free plan allows 30 orders per month with basic analytics and mobile access. For higher-volume traders, paid plans start at $29.95/month.

Does Trademetria have backtesting?

No. Trademetria has a PnL Simulator (which models different position sizes against past trades) but no true backtesting engine. For backtesting with historical market data, TradeZella offers unlimited sessions with 11+ years of data.

Does Trademetria have a mobile app?

Yes -- native iOS and Android apps. This is a genuine advantage over most competitors.

What is Trademetria's AI Coach?

An AI feature on the Pro plan ($39.95/mo) that analyzes your trading patterns and provides personalized recommendations. It's useful but less interactive than TraderSync's Cypher AI and less analytically deep than TradeZella's Zella Insights.

How many brokers does Trademetria support?

400+ -- covering all major retail platforms (TradeZella has 500+, TraderSync has 900+).

Does Trademetria support crypto?

Yes -- plus stocks, options, futures, forex, and CFDs. It supports the widest range of asset classes among major journals.

Is Trademetria good for beginners?

The free plan and mobile apps make it accessible for beginners. But there's no structured education. For learning-focused beginners, TradeZella's Zella University and 25+ strategy templates provide more guided development.

What are Trademetria's gamification features?

Trading challenges, achievements, and community competitions designed to keep traders engaged and accountable. Unique in the journal space but may not appeal to all trader profiles.

Does Trademetria have trade replay?

No. For trade replay, TradeZella offers 3 replay modes on all plans, and TraderSync offers Market Replay with up to 250ms precision.

Can I use Trademetria for mentoring students?

Yes -- through white-label admin dashboards that let you create branded monitoring for your trading community. It's a different approach from TradeZella's Spaces, which provides structured mentorship tools (trade-level feedback, full account access, unlimited students) within the platform.
